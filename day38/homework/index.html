simple omlette recipe

An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal.This classic omlette combines beatan eggs cooked to perfection,optionally filled with your choice of cheese,vegetables,or meaths.

Preparation time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt,to taste
  • peper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • optional fillings:sheese,diced vegetables,cooked meats,herbs

Instruction

  1. Beat the eggs:in a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are
    well mixed.You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omlette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tlit the
    pan to ensure the evenlyy coat the surface
  4. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly
    runny in the mddle, sprinkle your choosen fillings over one half of the omlette
  5. FOld and serve:As the omlette continues to cook,carefully lift one edge and fold it
    over the fillings.Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate
  6. Enjoy:Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
